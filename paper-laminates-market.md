<p><p>The paper laminates market is segmented into low pressure and high pressure paper laminates. Low pressure laminates are produced using lower temperatures and pressures, resulting in lightweight, cost-effective options suitable for furniture and cabinetry. In contrast, high pressure laminates are manufactured under elevated pressure and temperature, providing enhanced durability, resistance to abrasion, and a variety of decorative finishes, making them ideal for high-traffic surfaces and demanding applications. Both types cater to diverse consumer needs in interior design and construction.</p></p>
