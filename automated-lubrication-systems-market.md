<p><p>Automated lubrication systems enhance equipment efficiency through precise lubrication delivery. The grease lubrication system utilizes a thick, semi-solid lubricant, ideal for high-load applications, providing a reliable, long-lasting solution with less frequent maintenance. In contrast, the oil lubrication system employs liquid lubricants, ensuring efficient heat dissipation and better flow in high-speed operations. Both systems contribute to reduced friction, wear, and downtime, but their application choice depends on operational requirements, equipment design, and environmental conditions.</p></p>
