<p><p>High Intensity Laser Therapy (HILT) devices utilize various laser types for therapeutic applications. Solid-state lasers, powered by solid gain mediums, offer durability and efficiency, making them popular in clinical settings. Gas lasers, using gases as the active medium, provide specific wavelengths beneficial in certain treatments but may be bulkier. Liquid lasers, employing dye solutions, enable tunable wavelengths for diverse applications, though they require more maintenance. Each type serves unique therapeutic needs, driving growth in the HILT market.</p></p>
