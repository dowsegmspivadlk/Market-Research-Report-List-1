<p><p>The dry pumps market comprises several types, each with unique applications. Claw dry pumps use rotating claws to create a vacuum, offering high efficiency for industrial processes. Oil-less rotary vane dry pumps utilize sliding vanes for air compression without lubrication, making them suitable for clean environments. Rotary screw dry pumps employ helical rotors to generate vacuum, ideal for continuous operation. Additionally, the "others" category encompasses other technologies like diaphragm and scroll pumps, catering to niche applications in various industries.</p></p>
